Known Constraints & Mitigation

⚠ Important:
  • HF vapor release uncertainty: Etch rate may vary ±30% depending on humidity and HF age. Mitigation: calibrate on test die; provide min/max etch time range (40–50 min).
  • PZT epoxy bonding in vacuum: Outgassing may cause small voids in bond line. Mitigation: extend room-temperature cure to 24 hours before subsequent processing.
  • DRIE grass on sidewalls: May increase hinge damping by 10–30%. Mitigation: optimize SF6/C4F8 cycle ratio; post-DRIE O2 ash for cleaning.
  • Au migration in bond pads: Long-term reliability concern. Mitigation: use Ti barrier layer; store finished die at <60°C.
  • Yield targets: Wafer-level >85%, die-level >80%, mechanical >95%. Delamination rate target <2% (from PZT bonding step).
