# Module 03: Power Management — Requirements

## Questions to Answer

1. **Power Budget**: Total power (actuators + electronics + sensors)
2. **Battery vs. Wireless**: Tethered, battery, or wireless energy harvesting?
3. **Flight Time**: How long can robot fly on one charge?
4. **Weight Penalty**: How much does power system add to total mass?

## Inputs

- From Module 01: Actuator power draw
- From Module 02: Aerodynamic power
- From Module 04: Controller power

## Outputs

- Power budget summary (mW breakdown)
- Battery mass and capacity (mAh, mg)
- Flight endurance (minutes)

## Success Criteria

- Total power < 100 mW
- Flight time > 5 minutes (minimum practical)
- Battery mass < 10% of robot mass
