# Module 05: Structural Design — Requirements

## Questions to Answer

1. **Body Geometry**: Thorax size, leg attachment points
2. **Material**: Carbon fiber composite, titanium, or MEMS-silicon?
3. **Resonance**: Natural frequencies must avoid wing beat harmonics
4. **Stress**: Max stress under peak wing loads

## Success Criteria

- Resonance frequencies: 1st mode > 400 Hz (2× wing beat)
- Max stress < 100 MPa
- Total body mass < 5 mg
