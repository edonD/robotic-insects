# Module 06: Flight Dynamics — Requirements

## Questions to Answer

1. **Attitude Control**: Pitch/roll/yaw dynamics
2. **Stability**: Is the robot naturally stable or must controller stabilize?
3. **Control Loop**: Feedback gains, update rate, sensor requirements
4. **Maneuver Envelope**: What max accelerations can it achieve?

## Success Criteria

- Phase margin > 30°
- Gain margin > 6 dB
- Settling time < 1 second
- Control bandwidth > 20 Hz
