# Module 04: Control Electronics — Requirements

## Questions to Answer

1. **Microcontroller**: ARM Cortex-M0, RISC-V, or custom ASIC?
2. **Wireless Protocol**: Bluetooth, RF (2.4 GHz), acoustic
3. **Latency**: Control loop frequency (at least 100 Hz for stable flight)
4. **Power & Size**: Must fit on robot body (<1 mg)

## Success Criteria

- Latency < 10 ms
- Wireless range > 1 meter
- Power < 10 mW standby, 30 mW active
- Size < 2×2 mm
