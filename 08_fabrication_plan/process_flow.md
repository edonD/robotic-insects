# Fabrication Process Flow

## Step 1: MEMS Fabrication

1. Substrate: Silicon wafer, 100 mm diameter, <100> orientation
2. Deposit: 5 µm SiO2 (thermal oxidation)
3. Pattern: Photolithography (3 µm features)
4. Etch: DRIE (deep reactive ion etch) for body cavity
5. Release: HF vapor etch to free moving parts

## Step 2: Actuator Integration

1. Bond: Piezo stack to wing root (UV epoxy)
2. Electrical: Wire bonding for drive connections

## Step 3: Electronics Integration

1. MCU placement: Cortex-M0+ chip, flip-chip bonded
2. Wireless module: Small BLE antenna

## Step 4: Assembly & Test

1. Mechanical assembly: Wings snap-fit into body
2. Test: Flight characterization in wind tunnel

---

**Expected timeline**: 6 weeks for prototype batch
**Yield estimate**: 60% (first run)
