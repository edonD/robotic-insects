# Robotic Insect — Test Protocol

**Objective**: Validate design against specifications  
**Test Site**: Wind tunnel lab + open flight arena

---

## Test 1: Resonance Characterization

**Procedure**: Measure body resonance frequencies using accelerometer  
**Pass Criteria**: First mode > 400 Hz, away from wing harmonics  
**Equipment**: Shaker table, accelerometer, FFT analyzer

---

## Test 2: Wing Response

**Procedure**: Drive wing actuator with swept sine 100–300 Hz  
**Pass Criteria**: No instability, smooth response  
**Equipment**: Function generator, oscilloscope

---

## Test 3: Hovering Flight

**Procedure**: Release robot in calm chamber, record position via optical tracking  
**Pass Criteria**: Stable hover > 5 seconds  
**Equipment**: Wind tunnel, high-speed camera (500 fps)

---

## Test 4: Speed & Maneuverability

**Procedure**: Controlled flight through obstacle course  
**Pass Criteria**: Max speed 3 m/s, 10+ consecutive flips  
**Equipment**: Wind tunnel, IR markers, motion capture

---

## Test 5: Endurance

**Procedure**: Log flight time until battery depleted  
**Pass Criteria**: > 5 minutes continuous flight  
**Equipment**: Chronometer, wireless power monitor

---

## Test 6: Swarm Coordination

**Procedure**: Fly 5+ robots simultaneously, measure collision avoidance  
**Pass Criteria**: No collisions at 2 robots/m³ density  
**Equipment**: Multi-robot arena, acoustic/RF test system

---

**Timeline**: 2 weeks per test phase
