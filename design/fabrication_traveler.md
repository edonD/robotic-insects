---

## Material Stack

| Layer | Material | Thickness | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Substrate | Silicon <100> | 500 µm | Body structure |
| Insulator | SiO2 | 5 µm | Electrical isolation |
| Actuator | PZT-5H | 100 µm | Wing actuation |
| Metallization | Ti/Au | 0.5 µm | Electrical connections |

---

## Process Steps (Placeholder)

1. Clean substrate (RCA standard)
2. Thermal oxidation: 5 µm SiO2 @ 1000°C
3. Photolithography: body outline (3 µm features)
4. DRIE etch: 400 µm cavity for thorax
5. Piezo bonding: UV epoxy
6. Wire bonding: interconnects
7. HF vapor release: free moving parts
8. Dicing: singulation
